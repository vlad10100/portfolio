Mechanical Engineering Projects

Solar Chimney Power Plant

Solidworks

My 5th-year project study was the Small-Scale Solar Chimney Power Plant. It is a renewable energy power plant for generating electricity using the updraft air from the collector to propel the turbine. The heat is transferred from sensible heat storage material to the air that enters the collector via convection, the air is warmed, the density decreases, and an updraft air occurs.

For more information and to see the data you can visit scpp.vladimirben.com.

Bicycle Roller Trainer

Solidworks

The bicycle roller trainer is a commissioned project for a thesis about renewable energy by a famous TikTok artist CarbonLodi. The roller is connected using a rope to a motor-generator that generates electricity when the rollers of the trainer rotate. My job for this project is to create a 3D model for their project study with measurements and a bill of materials.

UV Disinfection Pen

Solidworks

The UV Disinfection Pen is a commissioned work for a project study. The purpose of the pen is to write and disinfect personal belongings using UV light. The pen is detachable for easy access to the internal components and wiring.The design was not perfect at first but through 3D model visualization, the design was optimized and finalized for fabrication.

Shell-Eco Urban Concept

Solidworks

I designed an urban concept car for Shell-eco Marathon 2020 in accordance with the rulebook. The car design was based on the shape of a droplet, which is considered to have a low aerodynamic drag that will result in less fuel consumption and easier car acceleration. The fabrication of the car is also considered while coming up with an aesthetically pleasing design.
